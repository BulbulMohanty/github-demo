The prototype of Hero Maestro electric has been revealed. 
t is Hero MotoCorp’s first electric scooter and expected to be launched in 2021.
Media reports have said that Hero MotoCorp’s first electric scooter will be based on the
Maestro 125. Now, the revelation of the prototype of the upcoming electric scooter,
which is called the eMaestro Proto, has solidified those speculations. As expected,

However, Hero MotoCorp has given it several interesting features to ensure that its first
